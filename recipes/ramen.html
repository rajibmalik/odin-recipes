Ramen

Description

Stir-fried ramen with chicken or your choice of meat. Using chopsticks makes cooking this super easy.

The trick to this recipe is to keep the food moving - especially the ramen. This recipe is fully customizable. I used soy sauce-flavored ramen. I also replace 1 tablespoon of ramen water with an equal amount of sake, this adds a phenomenal flavor. Fresh veggies can be used for best results, but I used frozen vegetables for the convenience. I also used pork bits for my meat but you could also use boneless, skinless chicken thighs, diced.
